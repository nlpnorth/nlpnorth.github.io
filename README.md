# nlpnorth.github.io
NLPnorth research group at ITU

The **NLPnorth** Natural Language Processing research group at the [Computer Science department](https://wiki.itu.dk/computerscience/index.php/Main_Page) at [ITU (IT University of Copenhagen)](http://www.itu.dk/) is a team of faculty, postdocs, PhD students, assistants and students who work together on fundamental and applied problems for computational understanding of human languages and speech.
