Call for Language Data Annotators

The NLPnorth group at the IT University of Copenhagen is seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented annotator to assist in our language data annotation tasks. The candidate will be responsible for annotating texts for a short-term Natural Language Processing (NLP) research project. This position is ideal for students with a background in linguistics, data science, computer science, or a related field who are interested in the intrinsic properties of language.

Responsibilities

  • Annotate text for an NLP research project;
  • Follow established annotation guidelines and protocols;
  • Ensure high-quality annotations by performing quality checks on the data;
  • Collaborate with group members to identify areas for improvement in annotation processes;
  • Maintain a high level of accuracy and attention to detail while meeting project deadlines;
  • Participate in team meetings and provide regular updates on progress.

Requirements

  • Fluency in the English language;
  • Excellent attention to detail and ability to maintain accuracy while annotating data;
  • Ability to work independently;
  • Familiarity with annotation tools and software is a plus;
  • Excellent communication and time-management skills;
  • Danish residency.

This is a part-time position, and the candidate will work approximately 8 hours per week for a maximum of 4 weeks. The salary for this position is based on an honorarium. This position offers an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in NLP and work with a team of experienced researchers who will be available to support the annotators throughout the process.

To apply, email a brief motivation and PDF resume to Rob van der Goot (robv@itu.dk).

NLPnorth is a research unit at the IT University of Copenhagen
