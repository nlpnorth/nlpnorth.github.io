Opportunities

Prospective PhD students and postdocs may consider applying for one of these opportunities for getting your own funding through one of the following calls:

  • DDSA visit grant
  • ITU PhD Open Callwith a deadline in Februari.
  • DARA PhD fellowship with a deadline in Februari.
  • DDSA PhD fellowships with a deadline in March.
  • DDSA Postdoc fellowships with a deadline in March.
  • Marie-Curie Postdoc fellowship for international applicants. Deadline in September, more More information.

These are all yearly calls, so if the deadline has passed, they are likely to be opened again in 2027. If you have an idea that matches the area of one of our full-time faculty, you are welcome to contact them directly. Please reach out introducing yourself and stating why you are interested in working with them specifically, as well as your background and any relevant experience.

Responsibilities

  • Annotate text for an NLP research project;
  • Follow established annotation guidelines and protocols;
  • Ensure high-quality annotations by performing quality checks on the data;
  • Collaborate with group members to identify areas for improvement in annotation processes;
  • Maintain a high level of accuracy and attention to detail while meeting project deadlines;
  • Participate in team meetings and provide regular updates on progress.
