Jobs

Prospective PhD students and postdocs may consider applying for a yearly opportunity for getting your own funding via DDSA fellowships (see 2023 call for PhD and postdoc fellowships), with application deadlines about February. If you have an idea that matches the area of one of our full-time faculty, you are welcome to them directly. Please reach out introducing yourself and stating why you are interested in working with them specifically, as well as your background and any relevant experience.
